Introduction

HistoricHarpsichord is a simple program to synthesize an historical harpsichord. It takes input from a USB MIDI keyboard and plays the notes on the audio output, with an optional soft damping effect.

1 Usage

Double click the HarpsichordSynthesizer icon to run the program, after connecting your MIDI keyboard.
If you have more than one keyboard, or other variations, double click the SetupHarpsichord icon. Check that the audio and MIDI settings are right. Here you can also choose a different sound font (Bank 0, program 0 only) choose a temperament or choose A=415 or make the damping abrupt.
